Jupiter:
_______________________________________________________
Position: 5th
Fun Fact: Largest planet — more than twice the mass of all other planets combined
Surface: No solid surface; made of gas (hydrogen and helium)
Notable Feature: Great Red Spot (a giant storm)
Moons: 95 confirmed moons — including Ganymede, the biggest moon in the solar system
Day Length: ~10 hours
Year Length: 12 Earth years
